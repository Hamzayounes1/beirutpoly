About BeirutPoly

BeirutPoly is a creative twist on the classic Monopoly board game, capturing the charm and resilience of Beirut. Explore neighborhoods, landmarks, and businesses while navigating challenges unique to the city.

Features

  • Iconic Locations: Hamra, Gemmayzeh, Mar Mikhael, and more!
  • Cultural Elements: Lebanese traditions, cuisine, and lifestyle.
  • Local Challenges: Traffic jams, power outages, and more.
  • Community Chest & Chance Cards with Beirut-inspired scenarios.
